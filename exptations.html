Leelaparab-1

January 21, 2018 to January 11, 2019
3-9pm, Dwip Gallery

Kazi Salahuddin Ahmed
Emran Sohel
Syed Mohammad Zakir

As a part of its journey through the existing ecology of contemporary art, 180 Degrees organized “Leelaparab-1” an art exhibition by Syed Muhammad Zakir and Emran Sohel. Mahbubur Rahman will be present as guests at the opening of the exhibition curated by artist and critic Mustafa zaman.
